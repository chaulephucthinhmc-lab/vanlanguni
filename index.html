Why Did We Create This Donation Fund? _We created this donation fund because plastic pollution is becoming a serious problem, while many people with disabilities need more job opportunities and support. We wanted to connect these two issues with one solution. By supporting people with disabilities who recycle plastic bottles, we can help reduce plastic waste and improve their lives at the same time. Where Will the Donations Go? _All donations will be used to support people with disabilities who collect and recycle plastic bottles. The money can help them buy recycling tools, transportation, safety equipment, and other materials needed for their work. It can also help improve their living conditions and create more opportunities for them to earn an income. How Can You Donate? _Donating is simple. Visit our website and click on the "Donate" button. You can choose the amount you would like to donate and follow the instructions on the screen. Every donation, big or small, can make a difference. Thank You for Your Support _Thank you for visiting our website and learning about this project. We truly appreciate your interest, support, and donations. Your contribution not only helps people with disabilities but also helps protect our environment from plastic waste. Together, we can create a cleaner world and a brighter future for everyone. Thank you for being part of this meaningful journey.
